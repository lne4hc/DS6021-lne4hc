The curvature visible in the residual plot suggests that a simple linear term does not fully capture the relationship.

Final Conclusion

The spline model captures the nonlinear relationship between age and wage more accurately than the simple linear regression model. Cross-validation generally shows a small improvement in predictive accuracy, while calibration remains good. Although the improvement is modest, the spline provides a more realistic representation of wage growth over a person’s career. Therefore, the restricted cubic spline model would be preferred over the simple linear model.
